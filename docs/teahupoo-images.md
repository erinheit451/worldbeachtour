# Teahupoʻo — Wikimedia Commons Image Inventory

Sourced via Wikimedia Commons category browsing and Special:MediaSearch.
All images below were individually fetched and license/author confirmed.
Last surveyed: 2026-04-19.

## Summary of availability

- **STRONG**: village/road signs, the wave from above (Ifremer aerial set), historical Polynesian canoe + Hawaiian heʻe nalu imagery, one Vahine Fierro photo at Teahupoo.
- **DECENT**: Laird Hamilton barrel shot (only iconic-ish surfer-in-wave Commons has), some 2019 landscape from village, satellite of full Tahiti Iti.
- **GAPS**: No Kauli Vaast portrait on Commons. No Olympic competition photos at Teahupoo (judges' tower, contest scenes, medal moments) — none uploaded under permissive license. No drone shots tagged as "drone." No Passe Havae reef ecology shots. No before/after of the controversial Olympic judges' tower. No clear hero "barrel with surfer perfectly framed" — Commons has aerial wave + Hamilton flat shot, but not the famous Hamilton "Millennium Wave" or the iconic barrel imagery (those are all rights-managed sports photography).
